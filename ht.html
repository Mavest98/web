    DJ MAVE$T

    DJ Mavest was first inspired by the legendary DJ Black Coffee, who showed him the power of electronic music to connect with people. I also cites Maphorisa and Kabza De Small as major influences, and their sound has helped shape my own style. As for my artist name, "Mavest" comes from the idea of "majestic music", and it's my goal to create tracks that will transport listeners to another place..
